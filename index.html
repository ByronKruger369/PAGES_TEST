Meet the Examiner

Before Polygraph Clarification Professional Services, Derick started his career in the South African Police Force in the Crime Prevention Unit; their aim was preventing and eradicating all street crime viz drugs, car thefts, house breaking, etc. This unit received a high success rate during the eighties. From this unit he was recruited to the Security Police Division; he also successfully completed the Bomb Disposal Expert Course, next to the Task Team one of the most sought after units in the Police Force.

In 1994, Derick entered the private sector obtaining various diplomas in Warehouse Management; Industrial Relations and Security Management. He built up experience in these fields by working for private companies; however, felt the calling to his original expertise and began doing investigations mainly but not limited to insurance claims. This was a stepping stone to where he finds himself today, Polygraph; as he saw the success rate Polygraph achieves and joined the profession.

He obtained his training from the Maryland Institute of Criminal Justice in the USA during 1997, which is an accredited training school recognized by the controlling body, American Polygraph Association. He periodically attends training to keep up to date with the latest techniques and advances in the polygraph industry.

He has been conducting Polygraph Examinations for more than 18 years, and has examined in excess of 12,000 individuals to date.

Polygraph Clarification Professional Services

Polygraph Clarification Professional Services was started after he returned from his training in the United States; he is an Associate Member, in good standing, of the American Polygraph Association (APA) as well as the South Africa Polygraph Federation (SAPFED).

He has on various occasions given testimony in internal hearings as well as at the Council for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA).
