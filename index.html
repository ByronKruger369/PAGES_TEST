Types of Tests

Pre-Employment Tests

These should be seen as part of a comprehensive and through check into the background of a prospective employee. These tests often serve to increase disclosure; deter prospective unsavioury applicants and detection of prospective employees who have not been honest on their application.

Periodic Screenings Tests

Screening tests are done when there has been a trend in losses however no specific dertails ie. date and time is not available. They can be bracketed under Company Loss Protection. Periodic Screenings of staff who work with valuable and or sensitive stock/detail can also act as a deterrent for any planned untoward activities.

Specific Incident Tests

Employees are tested for specific involvement/knowledge od an incident where dates and times are available pertaining to the specific event.
