Types of Polygraph Tests

Pre-Employment Tests

These should be seen as part of a comprehensive and thorough check into the background of a prospective employee. These polygraph tests often serve to increase disclosure; deter prospective unsavory applicants and detection of prospective employees who have not been honest on their application. Read more about these tests…

Periodic Screenings Tests

Screening polygraph tests are done when there has been a trend in losses however no specific details i.e. date and time are available. They can be bracketed under Company Loss Protection. Periodic Screenings of staff who work with valuable and or sensitive stock/detail can also act as a deterrent for any planned untoward activities. Read more about these tests…

Specific Incident Tests

Employees are tested for specific involvement/knowledge of an incident where dates and times are available pertaining to the specific event. Read more about these tests…
